# Irregular Verbs

These verbs do not follow standard patterns and must be memorized.

- ANDARE (to go)
- AVERE (to have)
- BERE (to drink)
- DIRE (to say)
- DOVERE (to have to)
- ESSERE (to be)
- FARE (to make, do)
- POTERE (can, to be able to)
- RIMANERE (to stay, remain)
- SAPERE (to know)
- SCEGLIERE (to choose)
- USCIRE (to go out, leave)
- VENIRE (to come, arrive)
- VOLERE (to want)
